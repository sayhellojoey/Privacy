Darcy Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Darcy is designed to help you track personal health and journey information while keeping your data private.

Data Stored on Your Device

Darcy stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Medication schedules and intake logs
  • Lab results and chart points
  • Appointments
  • Journey notes, photos, and videos
  • Profile fields you choose to enter (such as name, birthday, and pronouns)

All information is stored locally on your device.

Permissions Used

Darcy may request the following permissions to enable specific features:

  • Photos and Camera — used to add images or videos to journey entries.
  • Microphone and Speech Recognition — used for dictation and media capture.
  • Calendar — optional import of appointments.
  • Notifications — used for medication and appointment reminders.

You can change or revoke these permissions at any time in your device’s iOS Settings.

Data Collection and Sharing

Darcy does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on external servers.

  • Darcy has no remote access capability to your private data.
  • No backend account system is used for storing health records.
  • Your data remains on your device unless you choose to export it.
  • Darcy does not require account sign-in.

App unlock uses your device’s authentication system (Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode).

Data Security

Because your information is stored locally on your device, its protection depends on the security features of your device and operating system.

Darcy relies on iOS security features such as device encryption and authentication to help protect your data.

Your Controls

You remain in full control of your data:

  • You can export your data from Settings.
  • You can delete your local data from Settings.
  • You can manage permissions in iOS Settings.

Deleting the app will also remove all locally stored data.

Medical Disclaimer

Darcy is intended for personal tracking and organizational purposes only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Information stored in the app should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional regarding medical decisions.

HIPAA Notice

Darcy is a personal health tracking tool and is not a healthcare provider or covered entity under HIPAA. The app does not transmit health information to external servers or healthcare systems.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
